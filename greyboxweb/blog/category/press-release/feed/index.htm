Do you know what Scrum is? CEO & Founder of Grey-Box, Valentin Kravtchenko, shares his thoughts on how using this framework can be used to succeed in managing your team!

We know that project management can be overwhelming and can often consume more of your valuable time than doing the actual work itself. In a discussion with Anouare Abdou from The Ladders, Kravtchenko mentions how important it is to win your team’s heart by adopting a workflow your entire team can follow.
